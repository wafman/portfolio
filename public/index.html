About Me

My name is William Fritts. I am a Software Developer with a background in HTML/CSS/JavaScript and Java. I am a veteran and current member of the National Guard. I’ve always had an interest in software development. It stems from my love of science and desire to learn new things. At a young age, I fell in love with reading. As I grew older, that turned into a love of learning. My main interests have always been the sciences and I loved reading science fiction. I’ve looked to that future in those books as a guide to what we can be and have wanted to help bring that future to now. My skills in software development will allow me to help build applications and develop technologies which can better the lives of people. My time in the military has taught me that it’s not just people that need functional applications and technologies. My training can help not just bring the future to now for people, but for businesses as well.
